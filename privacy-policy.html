Privacy Policy

PostIt for macOS  ·  Last updated: June 3, 2026

Overview

PostIt is a floating sticky-notes app for macOS. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data the app handles and how.

Data We Do Not Collect

PostIt does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. Specifically:

  • We do not collect your name, email address, or any account information.
  • We do not track usage analytics or crash reports through third-party services.
  • We do not send any data to our servers or any third party.
  • The app has no network access and makes no internet connections.

Data Stored on Your Device

Your notes are saved locally on your Mac at: ~/Library/Application Support/PostItApp/notes.json

This file stays entirely on your device. It is never uploaded anywhere. You can delete it at any time to remove all notes.

iCloud

PostIt does not use iCloud or any cloud sync service. Notes are not synced across devices.

Third-Party Services

PostIt does not integrate with any third-party SDKs, analytics tools, or advertising networks.

Children's Privacy

PostIt does not collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, we will update the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

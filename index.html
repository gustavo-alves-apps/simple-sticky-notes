Privacy Policy

Simple Sticky Notes for macOS  ·  Last updated: June 16, 2026

Overview

Simple Sticky Notes is a floating sticky-notes app for macOS. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data the app handles and how.

Data We Do Not Collect

Simple Sticky Notes does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. Specifically:

  • We do not collect your name, email address, or any account information.
  • We do not track usage analytics or crash reports through third-party services.
  • We do not send any data to our servers — we do not operate any servers.

Data Stored on Your Device

Your notes are saved locally on your Mac at: ~/Library/Application Support/SimpleStickyNotes/notes.json

This file stays entirely on your device. It is never uploaded anywhere. You can delete it at any time to remove all your stickers.

iCloud

Simple Sticky Notes does not use iCloud or any cloud sync service. Notes are not synced across devices.

In-App Purchases

Simple Sticky Notes offers optional tips to support development, processed through Apple's In-App Purchase (StoreKit). All payment information is handled entirely by Apple — we never receive, see, or store any billing or payment details.

Third-Party Services

Simple Sticky Notes does not integrate with any third-party SDKs, analytics tools, or advertising networks. The only network activity in the app comes from Apple's own StoreKit framework when processing optional tips.

Children's Privacy

Simple Sticky Notes does not collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, we will update the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
